Ratio Test

The ratio test allows us to quickly identify the end behavior of a series by comparing the ratio of successive terms, \(a_{n+1}, a_n\) as n grows infinitely large. There are three cases of this comparison.

Let \( a_n = \frac{2^n}{n!} \)
Let \( a_n = n! \)
Let \( a_n = \frac{1}{n} \)
Let \( a_n = \frac{2^n}{n!} \)
Let \( a_n = n! \)
Let \( a_n = \frac{1}{n} \)
