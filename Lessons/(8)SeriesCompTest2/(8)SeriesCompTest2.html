Larger Series:

If a larger series converges, the smaller series converges also.

If a larger series diverges, the smaller series can diverge or converge.
Smaller Series:

If a smaller series converges, the larger series can diverge or converge.

If a smaller series diverges, the larger series must diverge as well.
Formal Statement:

For series with positive terms, if \( 0 \leq a_n \leq b_n \), then:
- If \( \sum b_n \) converges, so does \( \sum a_n \).
- If \( \sum a_n \) diverges, so does \( \sum b_n \).
